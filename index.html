How to play

Your task is to travel around European countries based on clues. There are three tips to help you, the first of which is free. If the first tip is not enough, you can buy two more tips.

You can get a list of countries that can be guessed by pressing the countries key. If you guess the country wrong three times, you get a fine and automatically fly to the correct country.

When you answer correctly, you have to catch the flying tiger.

You win the game if you can fly through all European countries.

The game ends if you run out of money.
